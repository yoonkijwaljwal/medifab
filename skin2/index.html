MEDIFAB Co., Ltd. is a global medical aesthetics and anti-aging company offering comprehensive solutions across aesthetics, therapeutics, and cosmetics.

The company's technology is built on three proprietary ingredient platforms — Chitogen™, Regentrix™, and Chitogenix™.

Chitogen is a water-soluble chitosan technology, Regentrix is a porcine-derived acellular dermal matrix known as dECM, and Chitogenix combines the two platforms to create added synergy.

R&D

Three Platforms,
One Molecular Foundation

Chitosan-based molecular engineering powers three distinct technology platforms — each validated through clinical evidence and global regulatory approval.

View All R&D
01.

CHITOGEN™

Chitosan-based injectable material that converts from liquid to gel within the body, delivering natural volume restoration with high biocompatibility and long-lasting effect.

02.

REGENTRIX™

A decellularized extracellular matrix (dECM) platform derived from natural biological sources. Engineered for tissue engineering and regenerative medicine, supporting wound healing and cellular regeneration.

03.

CHITOGENIX™

The next-generation evolution of CHITOGEN™, currently advancing through clinical trial IND stage. Targeting joint and ligament applications with government-backed R&D investment of over $3M.
