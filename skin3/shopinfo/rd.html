OVERVIEW

A smart, ion-responsive water-soluble
chitosan platform

Chitogen™ is MEDIFAB's proprietary technology that integrates specific chemical structures and phosphate ions into carboxymethylchitosan to achieve a physiological ion-responsive phase transition (Liquid-to-Gel). Upon injection, it responds instantly to body temperature and the ionic environment, transitioning from liquid to firm gel to provide immediate structural support. With 4.7× lower injection force than conventional HA fillers, it enables precise and comfortable application even through a 30G needle.

KEY MECHANISM

Liquid-to-Gel transition

Thermo- & ion-responsive — instant gelation upon injection, stable structure maintained

PERFORMANCE

vs. HA filler via 30G needle — precise application, reduced practitioner fatigue
